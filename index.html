About Us

This is what we are.

We are a group of hard working students that share similar goals and aspirations. Most of you might have goals right now such as learning about cyber, understanding cyber, gettting A's. These are all great goals for students in any field here at PPCC. One of the Cyber Clubs goals, is to help students reach their own potential. We do this by training Fridays, and in-person Mondays, we have chat rooms to help in Discord for many of the required classes. Not only that but we also encourage everyone to do competitions, and we will help you train to get ready for them.

What to expect from the Cyber Club

Bridge

The Cyber Club aims to be the Bridge between the academic knowledge we learn in class and the practical know-how of real cyber wars and server hacking.

Network

This Club will also continue building our vast network of entry-level and industry professionals. This gives us all a chance to see cyber from a different prespective while meeting respected people in the field.

Friendships

This does not mean that if you join the club you automatically have friends. Although, if you compete this semester and spend hours on in with someone you didn't know before, there's a good chance you could leave this school with a few friends, is all I'm saying.
