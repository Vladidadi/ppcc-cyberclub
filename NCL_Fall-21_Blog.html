National Cyber League: If your not sure, this is the competition for you!

August 4, 2021

Competitions News

Dust off those calendars, its getting to be that time of the year again and events are going to start popping up regularly!!

The first announcement is for the NCL Fall Competition! For those of you who haven't taken part in an NCL (National Cyber League) event before, now is your opportunity! We are looking for as many participants as we can get! The NCL is a national tournament that High Schools, 2-year and 4-year Colleges can all take part in.

It's not "too hard" for anyone.

Anyone, with any level of knowledge is welcome to come and compete. This competition looks great on resumes, and gives an official document stating your placement. It is a fantastic opportunity to experience a number of different aspects of Cybersecurity and many of those who have participated in the event have stated that they have learned a ton of great information and skills!

An additional education/training site can be found at CryptoKait's Blog which is a NCL Player Ambassador site geared to preparing participants for the NCL Tournament.

#NCL21Fall

Pikes Peak Cyber Club finished 32nd nationally during the spring competition and our goal is to keep that momentum going strong!! We all would encourage anyone who is interested to take part. You do not need any previous experience, just a desire to learn and have a great time!

NCL Fall Schedule

  • Here is the information on the dates for the Fall NCL Competition:
  • Registration: Aug 23 - Oct 8
  • Gym Opens: Aug 23 - Dec 17
  • Preseason Game: Oct 11 - Oct 18
  • Individual Game: Oct 22 - Oct 24
  • Team Game: Nov 5 - Nov 7

Don't be intimidated. NCL is made for beginners like us and can turn you into a pro. We look forward to competing with you!!

If you have any questions, or are interested in participating in the NCL event, please contact any of the club officers and we can answer any questions you may have!
